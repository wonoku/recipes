Prepare ahead with overnight oats! instructions

  1. Combine oats, seeds and nuts in a Tupperware container..
  2. Add finely grated chocolate if you have a sweet tooth!.
  3. Add chopped banana. This can also be mashed but I prefer slices..
  4. Add a table spoon of Greek yoghurt..
  5. Pour in the milk and mix thoroughly..
  6. Store in the fridge overnight and take with you on the go the next morning!.
