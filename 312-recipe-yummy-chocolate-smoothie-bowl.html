Ingredients of Chocolate smoothie bowl

  1. Prepare 2 of frozen bananas.
  2. It's 3 Tbs of cocoa powder.
  3. Prepare 5 Tbs of cold water/non-dairy milk.
  4. It's of Toppings.
  5. Prepare 1 of banana, fresh, sliced.
  6. Prepare 1 of dark chocolate jungle oats bar, cut into pieces.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of coconut flakes.
  8. You need 1 Tbs of freshly made cranberry berry jam(optional).
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of cacao nibs.

Chocolate smoothie bowl instructions

  1. Place frozen bananas, cocoa powder and cold water into a food processor. Blitz until smooth and creamy, about 3 minutes on high..
  2. Pour into serving bowl and place toppings on top. Toppings are according to preferance and may be changed to suit your taste. Enjoy..
