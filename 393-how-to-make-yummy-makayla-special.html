Makayla Special.

You can have Makayla Special using 9 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Makayla Special

  1. Prepare 4 of pork sirloin.
  2. It's 5 cups of all purpose flour.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of steak n shake seasoning salt.
  4. It's 2 Tbs of land of lakes butter.
  5. You need of About 1/4 cup oil.
  6. It's 2 of medium eggs.
  7. It's 1 cup of milk.
  8. It's of If you don't own steak n shake seasoning, use a mix of..
  9. It's of Garlic and onion seasoning, salt, touch of tumeric and paprika.

Makayla Special step by step

  1. Put oil in pan with butter and heat to medium.
  2. Place pork in a bowl mixed with eggs and milk already whisked up. Make sure to coat both sides of pork in egg wash mix.
  3. Place pork individually in a bag of flour with the steak n shake seasoning already mixed into the flour. Shake pork until it is fully coated.
  4. Place pork in sautee pan and season more steak n shake seasoning on top.
  5. Coat on both sides until golden brown.
  6. Make sure to check for doneness with meat thermometer.
  7. Place on plate and cut in half for glissening perfection.
