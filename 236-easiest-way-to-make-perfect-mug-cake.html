Ingredients of Mug Cake

  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of Coco powder.

  4. It's 1 of Egg.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of Oil.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Milk.
  7. It's 2-3 tbsp of Sugar.
  8. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla essence.

Mug Cake step by step

  1. In a mug add egg and beat until fuffly then add sugar,milk,oil or vanilla essence mix them until sugar desolved.
  2. In plate sift flour,Cocopowder or baking powder 3 times.
  3. Add them in the wet mixture slowly slowly mix well with slow speed.
  4. Then microwave for 2mins.
  5. Add milk chocolate on the top.It's ready to serve..
