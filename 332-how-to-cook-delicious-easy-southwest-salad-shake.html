Ingredients of Easy southwest salad shake

  1. You need 12 each of 1 pint canning jars.
  2. You need 1 packages of chopped iceberg lettuce or buy a head and chop.
  3. It's 4 each of tomatoes diced.
  4. Prepare 1 each of 12 oz bag frozen whole kernel corn.
  5. It's 1 can of can black beans rinsed.
  6. Prepare 1 can of pinto beans rinsed.
  7. You need 5 each of diced red, yellow, orange sweet peppers.
  8. Prepare 3 each of carrots shredded.
  9. Prepare 2 cup of shredded cheese.
  10. Prepare 1 each of bag of diced roasted chicken.
  11. You need 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro.
  12. Prepare 5 each of sliced radishes.
  13. It's 1 of your favorite type of spicy dressing.

Easy southwest salad shake instructions

  1. Individualize each ingredient in a row..
  2. Make sure you've washed your jars. Start with the lettuce on the bottom and go down the ingredient list adding layer by layer until the jar is just below the rim. Cilantro and radishes should be last..
  3. Cap and put in the fridge until ready to use. Add your favorite type of spicy dressing and enjoy!.
