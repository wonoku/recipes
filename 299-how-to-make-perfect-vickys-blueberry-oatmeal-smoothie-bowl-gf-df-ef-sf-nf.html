Ingredients of Vickys Blueberry-Oatmeal Smoothie Bowl, GF DF EF SF NF

  1. It's 200 g of gluten-free rolled oats.
  2. Prepare 250 g of blueberries.
  3. It's 10 of strawberries, hulled & chopped, save 2 for garnish.
  4. You need 1 of banana, peeled & chopped.
  5. It's 250 ml of light coconut milk.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of maple syrup / honey / agave nectar plus extra for serving.
  7. It's 2 tsp of vanilla extract.

Vickys Blueberry-Oatmeal Smoothie Bowl, GF DF EF SF NF instructions

  1. Put the oats in a bowl, cover with boiling water and let stand 5 minutes.
  2. Rinse and drain over a sieve and let cool.
  3. Throw into a blender with the blueberries (reserve a handful for garnish), strawberries, banana, coconut milk, syrup / honey and vanilla.
  4. Puree smooth.
  5. Pour into serving bowls and top with the reserved blueberries and sliced strawberries. Add some toasted, flaked almonds if you can tolerate nuts.
