Rhubarb overnight oats step by step

  1. Put the rhubarb in a large pan with the maple syrup and orange juice. Cook gently over low heat until the rhubarb is softened..
  2. In a bowl, mix the oats, cinnamon, yoghurt and half of the almonds..
  3. When the rhubarb is soft, drain the cooking liquid and keep for later..
  4. Add the rosewater to the rhubarb and mix gently..
  5. Add the cooking liquid from the rhubarb to the oats mix. Then add half of the rhubarb. Put the rest in a separate bowl. Put both bowls in the fridge overnight..
