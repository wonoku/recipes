Ingredients of Avocado and raspberry smoothie bowl

  1. It's 1 cup of baby spinach.
  2. Prepare 1 of large avocado.
  3. It's 1 of banana.
  4. It's 10 of mint leaves.
  5. You need 1 tablespoon of honey (optional).
  6. Prepare 10 of frozen raspberries.
  7. Prepare 2 of strawberries and mint for decoration.
  8. It's 1 cup of granola /muesli.
  9. You need 1 1/2 cups of coconut milk.
  10. You need 1 tablespoon of collagen.
  11. Prepare Pinch of Sesame seeds or any other you have.

Avocado and raspberry smoothie bowl step by step

  1. Peel the avocado and blend together with the mint, honey, 1 cup coconut milk, banana, baby spinach and collagen. Dish out and set aside..
  2. Take the raspberries and blend with the remaining coconut milk (1/2 cup)..
  3. Place the raspberry mixture in your bowl, add the avocado mixture on the side. Add the granola..
  4. Decorate as desired. One could use cocoa nibs, nuts, etc.
