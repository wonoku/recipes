Oats bhalla chaat step by step

  1. For bhalla:- remove all water from soaked oats and daal then grind oats and daal to make thin paste add salt and red chilli powder as ur taste and mix baking powder in it and deep fry like pakoras(size as u want).
  2. For imli chutney:- take a pan put imli paste and add 2 1/2 glass of water in it when ut comes to boil add jaggery salt and chilli powder and mix it boil it for 5 min then make corn flour paste with 4 spoon of water and mix it and add in chutney mix then it will become thick then switch off the gas.
  3. For green chutney:- grind all ingredients together then it's ready.
  4. Now take a pan fill it half with warm water with and dip bhallas in it for 5 min.
