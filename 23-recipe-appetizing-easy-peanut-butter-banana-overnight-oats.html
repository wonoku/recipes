Easy Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats.

You can cook Easy Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats using 8 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Easy Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats

  1. It's 1 large of ripe banana.
  2. You need 1 cup of Rolled or steel cut oats (do not use quick cooking!).
  3. It's 1 cup of Unsweetened original almond milk.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of Cinnamon.
  5. It's 1/2 tsp of Vanilla extract.
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp of Chia seeds.
  7. It's 1/4 cup of Creamy peanut butter.
  8. You need 1 tsp of Light agave syrup or honey (use more if you like sweeter oatmeal).

Easy Peanut Butter Banana Overnight Oats step by step

  1. In a large bowl mash your banana with a fork.
  2. Add remaining ingredients to bowl.
  3. Mix well, peanut butter must be evenly distributed!.
  4. Pour the mixture into 2 airtight containers or you can cover large bowl with plastic wrap, whichever you prefer..
  5. Refrigerate oats for 7 hours (overnight).
  6. When ready to eat, stir the oats.
  7. Add toppings of your choice like sliced bananas, shaved dark chocolate or chips :D you can also add a little splash of almond milk if you feel it's too thick!.
