Ingredients of Apples & Cinnamon Overnight Oats

  1. It's 1/2 of Apple (sliced and diced).
  2. It's 1 Pinch of Ground Clove.
  3. You need 1 Pinch of Ground Nutmeg.
  4. Prepare 1/2 TSP of Ground Cinnamon.
  5. It's 1/2 TBSP of Chia Seeds.
  6. Prepare 1 TBSP of Maple Syrup (or a sweetener of your choice).
  7. Prepare 1/4 Cup of 1 Minute Oats.
  8. Prepare 1/2 Cup of Milk (soy, almond, cow, etc.).

Apples & Cinnamon Overnight Oats instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in a mason jar (or container of your choice) in the order listed. Stir or shake container to mix ingredients..
  2. Refrigerate overnight or at least 6 hours. Enjoy cold, straight from the fridge, or transfer to a bowl and heat in the microwave!.
  3. (P.S. It's great hot or cold. I normally enjoy hot oats, but this recipe is good cold with a hot cup of coffee!) =].
