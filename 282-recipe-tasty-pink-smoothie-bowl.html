Pink Smoothie Bowl step by step

  1. In a tray spread banana and watermelon. Freeze for an hour at least..
  2. Add frozen fruits and condensed milk in a blender and blend on low speed. Until it gets grainy..
  3. Add almond/coconut milk, blend again on low speed, scraping down the sides as needed. Add more milk if required until the mixture reaches a soft-serve consistency..
  4. Scoop into individuals serving bowls or in a bigger bowl..
  5. Top it up with the toppings of your choice. Chia seeds, granola, diced fruit, seeds, dry fruits are good options. I have topped it up with chopped pistachio along with banana and strawberry. Added a drizzle of condensed milk..
  6. Serve immediately but leftover can be frozen and thawed before reusing..
