Meatloaf for 1, with Glaze step by step

  1. Mix everything, except, cheese and glaze ingredients..
  2. I use a large fork, no slimy hands, plus, integrated better!.
  3. Gently, don't want to overwork the mixture..
  4. Make glaze, mix ingredients, gently simmer. 15 minutes.
  5. Take half, of meatloaf mixture, make a bed for the cheese!.
  6. Put the cheese into the shallow bed!.
  7. Put the other half over the top..
  8. Make sure that you close the edges!.
  9. Other wise, the cheese will melt,and ooze out..
  10. Heat oven to 375 degrees.
  11. Spread sauce over meatloaf, save some for dipping..
  12. I baked for 45 minutes.
  13. Mashed potatoes and green beans were my sides.
