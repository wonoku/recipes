Overnight Oats Topped With Strawberries and Grapes instructions

  1. Add oats in bowl.
  2. Add in cinnamon stir to combine.
  3. Add honey and pour in the milk, stir until mixed well.
  4. Put oats mixture in the fridge overnight.
  5. Eat the next morning topped with fresh strawberries.
