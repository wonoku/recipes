Ingredients of Mango Chia Protein Shake

  1. It's 1/2 of Mango.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of Blueberries.
  3. You need 1 of Chia Seeds.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Vanilla Protein Powder.
  5. It's 1 tbsp of Chobani Greek Yogurt.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of Almond Milk.

Mango Chia Protein Shake instructions

  1. Add Mango to blender.
  2. Add Blueberries to blender.
  3. Add Chia Seeds and Chobani Greek Yogurt to blender.
  4. Add Vanilla Protein Powder to blender.
  5. Add Almond Milk to blender.
  6. Blend and Enjoy!.
