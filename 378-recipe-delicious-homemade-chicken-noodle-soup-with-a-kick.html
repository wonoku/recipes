Ingredients of Homemade chicken noodle soup, with a kick.

  1. You need 6 of boneless chicken thighs.
  2. Prepare 3 of boneless chicken breasts.
  3. It's 2 of large boxes of Swansons chicken broth.
  4. It's 4-5 stalks of celery.
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp of garlic salt.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of lemon pepper seasoning.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of blackening or cajun seasoning.
  8. You need 5-6 of shakes of Tabasco.
  9. Prepare 2 of Large packages of Grandmas Egg Noodles (in the frozen section).

Homemade chicken noodle soup, with a kick. step by step

  1. In a large stock pot cook the chicken completely covered with water, thus will start a stock for you...
  2. Add all of the seasonings and cook for 2 hours.. stir so the meat doesnt stick. Once the meat is cooked and can be shredded it is time to add the 2 large boxes of chicken broth. Bring back to a boil..
  3. Once boiling again add the 2 large packages of Grandmas Egg Noodles and cook on low stir often for 1 more hour or till the noodles are done..
