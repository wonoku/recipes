Ingredients of Simple Cabbage Rolls

  1. It's 20 of leaves Cabbage.
  2. Prepare 200 grams of Thinly sliced pork.
  3. It's 5 of to 6 shakes each ● Salt and pepper.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of ● Grated garlic.
  5. It's 1 of Consomme stock cube.
  6. It's 250 ml of Water.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of or more Salt.

Simple Cabbage Rolls instructions

  1. Remove the cabbage leaves one at a time and microwave or parboil. (Slice off the protruding part of the stalk, if desired.) Thoroughly rub the ● ingredients into the sliced pork..
  2. With the base of the stalk facing towards you, place 1 slice of pork on top of the cabbage leaf. Roll 2 or 3 times, then fold in the sides and continue rolling (In our home, we don't secure the rolls with a toothpick.).
  3. Arrange the rolls tightly in a pot, pour water with the dissolved consomme cube, then boil over medium heat. While boiling, remove any scum that may rise to the top, then cover with a drop lid (otoshibuta) and continue to simmer over low heat for 20 minutes. Sprinkle with salt or other spices of your choice and serve..
