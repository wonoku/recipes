Skipjack Tuna with Teriyaki Sauce instructions

  1. First, the sauce, combine the ingredients marked with ◎, microwave for 20 minutes at 500 W, and blend well..
  2. Slice the seared slapjack tuna into 6-7mm thicknesses, and sprinkle with black pepper..
  3. Put the tuna in a bowl, mix in the mayonnaise, sprinkle with flour, and coat evenly..
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan, and over medium high heat, cook both sides of tuna from Step 3 until golden brown..
  5. Turn off heat and soak up excess oil with a paper towel..
  6. Return to medium-high heat, add 3 tablespoons of sauce from Step 1, then remove from heat once the tuna is coated evenly..
  7. Arrange on serving plate, drizzle 1 tablespoon of sauce from Step 1 on top, and garnish with scallions and bonito flakes..
  8. Serve with sauce so that you can adjust to taste while eating..
  9. Add your choice of greens to the side for a good balance, and serve..
