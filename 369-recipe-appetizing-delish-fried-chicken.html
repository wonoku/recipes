delish fried chicken step by step

  1. add flour,seasoning,pepper,and garlic salt into bag, shake up to mix.
  2. mix milk and egg together in a bowl.
  3. take your chicken few at a time mix and shake up in bag of flour and seasonings.
  4. take flour coated chicken and dip into egg/milk. then shake it into bag and coat with flour and seasonings again.
  5. fry in pan with oil, brown on both sides turn down heat and cook for approximately 40 to 50 mins depends on how high ur heat is.
