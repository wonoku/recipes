Italian chicken instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350Â° f.
  2. in a bowl add sour cream and pepper (about 2 tsp).
  3. in seperate bowl add 1 package of shake 'n bake (there's two per box) with parmesan cheese.
  4. spread sour cream mixture to chicken on both sides.
  5. dip chicken and cover both sides with crumbs.
  6. place in oven pan.
  7. bake for about 30 min.
  8. then add cheese on top of each chicken breast and leave in the oven for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. enjoy :).
