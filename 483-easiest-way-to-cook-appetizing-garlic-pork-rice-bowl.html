Garlic Pork Rice Bowl step by step

  1. Mix the ☆ ingredients together.
  2. Cut the pork into small cubes, and season lightly with salt and pepper..
  3. Heat some oil in a frying pan and stir fry the pork until browned on the surface..
  4. Lower the heat and add the combined sauce ingredients to the pan, and stir-fry..
  5. Put the mixture on top of hot rice, sprinkle with sesame seeds and it's done..
