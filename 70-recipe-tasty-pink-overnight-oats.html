Pink Overnight Oats instructions

  1. In a blender, add chopped strawberries and honey. Blend to make a smooth paste..
  2. Add this puree to a bowl. Pour milk and yoghurt. Then combine everything together..
  3. Add the oats and give it a good mix. Cover the bowl with a lid and keep it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight..
  4. Serve the pink overnight oats in a bowl or jar and garnish with pomegranate arils..
