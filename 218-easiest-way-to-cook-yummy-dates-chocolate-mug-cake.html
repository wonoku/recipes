Ingredients of Dates chocolate mug cake

  1. It's 1 cup of chopped dates.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of coco powder.
  3. You need 3 tsp of maida/All-purpose flour.
  4. It's 3 tsp of oil.
  5. It's 3 tsp of milk.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  7. You need 1/3 tsp of baking powder.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of lukewarm water.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of chopped dryfruits.
