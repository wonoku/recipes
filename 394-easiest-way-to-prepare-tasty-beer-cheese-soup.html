Beer cheese soup step by step

  1. Melt butter, medium heat, in large Stock pot.
  2. Saute vegetables 4 -6 minutes.
  3. Except potatoes and green onions.
  4. Add spices and bacon, don't forget to reserve half cup bacon.
  5. Add chicken broth, beers, Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Add potatoes, bring to a boil.
  7. When potatoes are softened lower heat to a simmer.
  8. Mix grated cheese with the flour,add stir until combined.
  9. Add half and half, Do not Boil, keep at a simmer, will curdle.
  10. Adjust spices, add more to taste,.
  11. NO Salt, the bacon is the salt.
  12. Add smoked sausage.
  13. Simmer until well heated, or longer.
  14. The longer it simmers the better.
  15. This brings the spices out.
  16. Don't forget to have a beer.
  17. Ladle into bowls.
  18. Garnish with sour cream, green onions, reserved bacon.
  19. Make it your own, I also added sliced jalapenos.
  20. Serve with jalapeno cheddar cheese bread.
  21. I got mine from the jewel bakery.
  22. Awesome, cheap, no work, gives you time to drink your beer.
  23. Sam Adams would approve.
