Extremely Easy Lasagna step by step

  1. Meat Sauce: Cook all of the meat sauce ingredients in the order listed. Simmer while stirring until the liquid evaporates..
  2. White Sauce: Put the flour and butter into a heat-resistant dish and microwave for 1 1/2 minutes. Stir and then add the milk and flavoring ingredients. Combine well and microwave for 6 1/2 minutes..
  3. Assemble: Boil the lasagna so that it is still slightly hard. Layer the lasagna, meat sauce, lasagna, white sauce, in that order, into a dish. Repeat as necessary and then top with cheese..
  4. Bake: Place the layered lasagna on an iron tray and place on the middle rack in the oven (this will allow it to brown nicely). Bake for 20 minutes at 250°C..
