Ingredients of Keto mug cake

Keto mug cake instructions

  1. Measure the dry ingredients into a mug or jelly jar and mix completely with a fork..
  2. Add the egg, vanilla essence and water, stirring completely making sure to get it all from the bottom. Let batter sit 1-2 minutes..
  3. Microwave for 50 seconds, depending on your microwave. Decorate with whipped cream, raspberries and low carb chocolate sauce..
