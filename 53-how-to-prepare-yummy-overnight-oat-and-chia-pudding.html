Ingredients of Overnight oat and chia pudding

  1. Prepare 2 tbsp of chia seeds.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of rolled oats.
  3. Prepare Handful of Nuts of your choice.
  4. You need 2 tsp of honey.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of almond milk or full fat milk.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of choice of berries.
