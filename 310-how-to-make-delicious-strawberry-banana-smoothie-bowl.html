Strawberry-banana smoothie bowl instructions

  1. Take a banana. slice it. Put into a air tight box and freeze for about 10-20 mins.
  2. Slice the strawberries and freeze them too..
  3. Take out the frozen fruits and put them in a blender.
  4. Add About 2 tbs of milkmaid.
  5. Add 4-5 drops of vanilla essence.
  6. Blend it all into a smooth paste.
  7. Take the smoothie out into a bowl.
  8. Add toppings now. You can add granola, fresh strawberries and shredded pineapple..
  9. Enjoy your healthy and very tasty strawberry banana smoothie!.
