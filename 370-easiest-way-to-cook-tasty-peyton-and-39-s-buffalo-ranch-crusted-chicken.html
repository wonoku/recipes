Peyton's buffalo ranch crusted chicken step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix all sauces in bowl by its self.
  3. Pour the shake n bake in separate bowl.
  4. Dip chicken in sauce you can marinate how ever long you want then dip the chicken in the shake n bake..
  5. Put chicken on pan with foil.
  6. Bake chicken for 45 minutes at 400 degrees; depending on thickness of the chicken..
  7. Add cheese in the last 5 minutes of cooking if u want..
