Cacao smoothie bowl instructions

  1. Add the frozen banana, cacao powder, almond milk and dates into your blender (I use a nutribullet).
  2. Blend until smooth and pore into a glass or bowl depending on the consistency..
  3. Top with cacao nibs, goji berries and banana slices.
  4. Enjoy!.
