Ingredients of Eggless Chocolate Rum Mug cake

  1. Prepare 8 tbsp of flour.
  2. It's 3 tbsp of granulated sugar.
  3. You need 3 tbsp of Cocoa powder.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of baking powder.
  5. Prepare 6-7 tbsp of milk.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of oil.
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of chocolate chip.
  9. It's 1 tbsp of Rum.

Eggless Chocolate Rum Mug cake instructions

  1. Take a big Mug, if your mug is small use above quantity in half.
  2. Add flour,granulated sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder and mix it well.
  3. Then add milk, oil, vanilla extract and rum and mix it well and make nice batter.
  4. Add chocolate chip on the top and put the cup in Microwave for 1.30 Min..
  5. Take the cup out and using the tooth pic check the cake is ready or not.if not then again put inside for another 20 sec.
  6. Decorate your mug cake with some fresh fruits and dust some sugar..
  7. To add more flavors you can add maple syrup..
  8. Your valentine Rum Treat is ready just in 3 minuets..
