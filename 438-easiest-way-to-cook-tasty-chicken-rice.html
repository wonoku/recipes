Ingredients of Chicken Rice

  2. It's 1/2 of Onion.

  4. You need 2 of rice bowls' worth Hot cooked rice.

Chicken Rice instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and sauté the cut chicken breast until it changes color. Add the finely chopped onions, sauté, and season with salt and pepper..
